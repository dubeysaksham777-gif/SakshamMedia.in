Delhi Opens Online Admissions for 12 Government Special Schools from June 11

By Saksham Dubey

New Delhi | June 10, 2026

Pic Courtesy: The Somaiya School

New Delhi, June 10: The Directorate of Education (DoE), Government of Delhi, has started the online registration process for admission to 12 government special schools across the national capital from June 11. The admission drive aims to provide specialized educational support to children with diverse learning and developmental needs.

According to the Education Department, parents and guardians can submit applications through the official admission portal. The special schools cater to students requiring specialized educational facilities and support services, ensuring inclusive and accessible education.

Applicants are advised to carefully review eligibility criteria, seat availability, and required documents before completing the registration process. Following the online application stage, document verification and admission formalities will be conducted as per the schedule issued by the department.

The initiative is part of the Delhi government's efforts to strengthen inclusive education and expand access to quality learning opportunities for children with special educational needs. Interested parents can access detailed guidelines through the official Directorate of Education website.
