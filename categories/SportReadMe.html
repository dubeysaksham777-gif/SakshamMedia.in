Ben Stokes Faces Uncertain Test Future Amid Nightclub Controversy

By Saksham Dubey

New Delhi | June 9, 2026

England captain Ben Stokes faces growing uncertainty over his Test career as an ECB investigation into a nightclub controversy continues.

Pic Courtesy: X (Twitter)

June 09: England Test captain Ben Stokes is reportedly considering stepping away from Test cricket and relinquishing the captaincy following a nightclub controversy that has shaken English cricket. Reports suggest Stokes and teammate Gus Atkinson are under investigation after allegedly breaching team curfew rules following England's first Test victory over New Zealand.

The fallout has already had major consequences, with both players being dropped from England's squad for the second Test. The England and Wales Cricket Board confirmed that disciplinary proceedings are ongoing, while former captain Joe Root has been appointed interim skipper.

to multiple reports, Stokes is weighing his future as captain, and speculation has emerged that he could even retire from Test cricket if the situation continues to escalate. However, no official announcement regarding retirement has been made by the player or the ECB.

The controversy has sparked debate over discipline and team culture within the England setup, with senior officials reportedly considering stricter conduct policies in the aftermath of the incident.
